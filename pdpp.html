Oak Park Township offers temporary handicapped parking cards to community residents with disabilities, for a period of up to 90 days to assist those with short-term needs and while they apply for permanent handicapped parking placards.

  • Your doctor must complete the Oak Park Township application
  • Temporary parking cards are valid for three months
  • Applications for permanent disability placards from the Secretary of Stateâ€™s office are also available

Applications for both temporary and permanent placards are available at the main Township office reception desk.

One of the ways the Township of Oak Park works to support residents with disabilities is by making medical equipment available whenever possible. Residents of Oak Park and River Forest may borrow equipment, with no time limit, such as:

  • Canes
  • Walkers
  • Shower chairs
  • Wheelchairs

All equipment is donated. The Township accepts donations of all medical equipment in good condition.

To find out about additional equipment availability, borrow and/or donate, please contact or visit the Township office.

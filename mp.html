Oak Park Township offers several meal programs to residents of Oak Park and River Forest age 60 and over. Qualifying residents can choose from:

  • Senior Lunch Program, which provides complete well-balanced meals, opportunity for social interaction, and educational programming. Lunch is served daily from 11:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. in the 1st floor dining room at 130 S. Oak Park Avenue, Oak Park.  There is a suggested donation of $1.50 per meal.
     
  • Home Delivered Meal Program, offering nutritionally balanced meals to qualified homebound residents. Please call a Senior Services Intake Coordinator at 708-383-8060 to enroll in the program.
     
  • Dine Out Program, giving seniors the opportunity to enjoy an affordable, well-balanced meal at specified local restaurants. For more information about the Dine Out Program please call Senior Services at 708-383-8060.  Coupons may be purchased for $4.00 each on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m. 
     

A calendar of menus and events is published in our quarterly newsletter and available online. Please call us at 708-383-8060 to be added to our mailing list or email us.  For a PDF version of the current lunch menu, please click here.  A donation is suggested for each meal, however no one is denied service based on ability to pay. 

For more information about senior meal programs, please contact Senior Services at 708-383-8060, or use our "Contact Us" form and select Senior Services for general questions.

Employment Opportunities

Thank you for your interest in employment opportunities with the Oak Park Township. Oak Park is a great place to live, work and play. We often post new opportunities, so please check back.

Oak Park Township is an Equal Opportunity Employer and is committed to diversity in the workplace. We offer numerous benefits including:

  • Competitive compensation
  • Emphasis on work-life balance
  • Dedication to providing quality services to residents
  • Opportunities for advancement and professional development

When a position is open at Oak Park Township we will post it here, and will place an announcement in the local press. If you are interested in a particular position, please submit a resume, cover letter and salary requirements to the staff listed in the announcement via email.

Current Employment Openings: 

Senior Services Case Manager - Full Time
 

Oak Park Township is seeking a full time Case Manager to serve as a member of the Senior Services team to help the Township achieve its mission of: 1) addressing the needs of our community by providing comprehensive assessment of the needs of older adults to develop a care plan to ensure their needs are met; 2) providing information, advocacy, assistance, counseling, crisis management and referrals for older adults to remain living independently in the community as long as possible; 3) collaborating with community partners to develop service plans and ensure the needs of the older adults are met.
 
The Case Manager will be responsible for:
  • Providing coordination and management of services to older adult clients in the elder abuse, Community Care, and geriatric case management programs.
  • Conducting comprehensive assessments according to program regulations and standards.​
  • Engaging with staff, volunteers, and program participants and utilizing information gained to meet the changing needs of the older adults.
The position requires a candidate with a Bachelor’s degree in social work, or a registered nurse and at least one year experience in service provision to older persons such as assessment, direct service, or authorization of formal service for the elderly. Experience with Microsoft Office, databases and applicant tracking systems is also required.  The full job description may be found here.
Preferred Applicants will have the following:
  • Valid Illinois Driver’s License and liability insurance
  • Knowledge of Title III Older American’s Act Services, Medicaid, Medicare, the Community Care Program, Social Security and Social Security Disability and other community services
  • Practical knowledge of the social, psychological and physiological aspects of aging
Oak Park Township is a unique unit of Oak Park local government, providing human services to vulnerable citizens of our community: at-risk youth needing guidance to improve their chances for a successful life, senior citizens needing support to maintain their independence and financially struggling residents who need monthly assistance and job seeking help. In addition, the Township provides funding for mental health agencies and assistance with property tax inquiries and appeals.
 
 
Interested applicants should submit a resume via email by March 18th, 2018 to Bianca Ingwersen, bingwersen@oakparktownship.org.
 

Oak Park Township is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

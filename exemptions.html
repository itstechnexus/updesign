Applications for Senior Property Tax Exemptions have been mailed.  All seniors must apply each year to renew their exemptions.  For more infomation click here.

All veterans who receive the Disabled Veterans Exemption are entitled to property tax savings.  A new law in 2016 allows veterans of the US Armed Forces who were severely wounded and while serving their country and who are at least 70% disabled complete exemption from property taxes.  Renewal applications are available on the Cook County Assessor website, or you may download the form and additional information here.  For eligibility rules and details from Township Assessor Ali ElSaffar, please click here.
